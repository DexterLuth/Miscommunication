## Stage 1: What is the customer asking for?

### Customer Intent

The customer is seeking **commercial financing** to support two primary objectives:

1.  **Immediate Business Expansion.**
2.  **Maintaining sufficient future leverage/flexibility** to execute future acquisitions, requiring careful negotiation of loan covenants.

The customer is currently in the information-gathering stage, seeking a **realistic structure, pricing, collateral requirements, constraints (covenants), and timeline** before formally submitting a complete application. They have ultimately decided to move ahead with the information request to initiate the process.
