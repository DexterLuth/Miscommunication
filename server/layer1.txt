**Customer Intent:**
The customer is seeking to **restructure and consolidate approximately $28,000 of high-interest debt** (ranging from 14% to 19%) into a more manageable financial product. Their primary goal is **predictability and a fixed payoff schedule** (knowing exactly when the balance will hit zero), leading them to favor a personal loan over a line of credit.

**Key Keywords & Metrics:**
*   Restructure debt
*   Debt consolidation
*   $28,000 (total amount)
*   14% to 19% (current rates)
*   Line of Credit (LOC)
*   Personal Loan
*   Predictability (as the primary priority)
*   Fixed payoff timing
*   Monthly payment
