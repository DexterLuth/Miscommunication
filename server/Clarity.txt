### Conclusion on Clarity

The banker excels in clarity by:

1.  **Structuring the Conversation:** Covering all critical aspects (structure, costs, collateral, covenants, constraints, timeline) in a logical, step-by-step manner.
2.  **Using Specific Regulatory Language:** Correctly naming **CIP/AML, Beneficial Ownership, Privacy Policy, Regulation B,** and **Leveraged Lending Guidance** to explain why certain information is required or why certain terms are constrained.
3.  **Setting Realistic Expectations:** Clearly differentiating non-binding discussions from commitments and anchoring non-negotiable terms (covenants, leverage limits) in regulatory guidance rather than pure internal preference.

The explanation is not misleading or contradictory to regulations; rather, it uses regulatory requirements as foundational elements of the discussion, achieving a high degree of transparency.
