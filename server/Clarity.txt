*   **Banker Statement:** "under our Customer Identification Program and anti-money laundering rules, we have to verify your identity... For a company, we also must identify and certify all beneficial owners who hold 25 percent or more, plus one control person."

#### 3. Privacy Policy (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act – GLBA)
*   **Banker Statement:** "any financial and personal information you provide will be handled under our Privacy Policy, as required by federal law."
*   **Compliance Check:** **Highly Compliant.** This acknowledges the obligation under GLBA to protect Non-Public Personal Information (NPI) and provide access to the Privacy Policy.

#### 4. Credit Decisions (Regulation B – Equal Credit Opportunity Act)
*   **Banker Statement:** "once you submit a completed application and all required documents, we are required under Regulation B to give you a credit decision within 30 days, along with a written notice if we decline or modify the request."
*   **Compliance Check:** **Highly Compliant.** Regulation B requires creditors to notify applicants of action taken on a credit application within 30 days of receiving a completed application, and provide an adverse action notice detailing the reasons for denial or modification. This is accurately explained, even for commercial applicants.
